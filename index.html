Alina Kubiv was born in Vinnytsia, in the middle of Ukraine. She received her art education at the Lviv National Academy of Arts, Department of Restoration of Works of Art. Worked as a restorer in the Museum of the History of Religion, further continued to work as a drawing and painting teacher for future designers and architects.
Alina Kubiv is happy to paint colorful flowers with bright and expressive strokes. Draws for herself for the joy of herself, for the joy of people. Draws to make the world more colorful. So that there will be more flowers in the world.
